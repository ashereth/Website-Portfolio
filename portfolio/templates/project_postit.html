After learning how to develop websites and following allong the course 'Web Developer Bootcamp with Flask and Python in 2023' by Jose Salvatierra, I decided to make this project to test my new skills. This website is a simple social media style app where users can create accounts, login, and make posts. After logging in users can also follow other accounts that they can find on the explore page. The explore page displays all posts by all accounts. Once the user has followed at least one account they will be able to see the posts from the accounts they follow on their following page.

I didn't spend much time writing css to make this website look good on mobile so for the best experience please access this website from a computer with a large screen. Enjoy :).
